# Hierarchy Quick Summary

\- Admin → Full Power\
- Leader (President) → Everything except user passwords & contact
messages\
- Vice Presidents → Control over their department only\
- Coordinators → Role-limited department control\
- Members → Basic viewing rights (no admin controls)

# Notes

\- Only HR Coordinators can add/remove members under them. They cannot
change roles of VPs or other Coordinators.\
- Event, Marketing, and Tech have limited-scope powers within their
functions.\
- Departments like Finance and Design currently have no active
responsibilities -- these can be updated later.
